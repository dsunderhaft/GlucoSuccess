MyHealth encrypts your data, separates it from your identity, and adds your data to a secure research database. This will create an unprecedented crowd-sourced database of health behaviors and lab values from people just like you. Studying all this real-world data will help researchers better understand the complex relationships between diet, exercise, and activities for people with a rare adipose disorder. It will also help explore how the iPhone can enable new kinds of clinical research.

Because MyHealth is both an app and a research study, we will explain the risks and benefits of the study as part of an informed consent process.
