COPD Research provides personalized insights into how your health behaviors relate to Rare Adipose values, to help you manage your disease. In general, richer data entered into the app results in more accurate and informative insights.

To drive these insights, RADSResearch will ask you to:

  • Answer survey questions about your COPD conditions (described more in a subsequent screen)
  • Collect your lab values

The app sends occasional reminders to complete study activities.
