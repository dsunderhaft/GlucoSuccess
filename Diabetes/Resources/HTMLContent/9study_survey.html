RADSResearch will collect some of its data on your health behaviors through short survey questions, such as:

  • Over the past week, did you take some or all of your medications?
  • How frequently you used compression, whole body vibration, lymph pumping, etc...?
  • Did you inspect your lipomas today?

Occasionally, there will also be longer surveys that evaluate aspects such as your quality of life, or your sleep duration and quality.

For dietary information, RADSResearch will ask you to use the free “Lose It!” app to log what you eat for meals.

In general, more data entered into the app results in more accurate and informative personalized insights .
