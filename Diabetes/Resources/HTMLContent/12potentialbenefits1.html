One of the biggest challenges for people with a RADS is tracking diet, exercise and other health behaviors. RADSResearch may help by streamlining these daily tracking tasks.

RADSResearch will also provide personalized insights and feedback to help you understand how your health behaviors can influence your RADs values. This may help you manage your RADs.

More generally, patients with a RADS may ultimately benefit from this research, because RADSResearch and its research study will create an unprecedented crowd-sourced database of health behaviors and RADs values from people just like you. Studying all this real-world data will help researchers better understand the relationships between diet, exercise, and activities in real-world people with RADs. It will also help explore how the iPhone can enable new kinds of clinical research.
